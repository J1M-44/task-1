About Me

My name is Jim Aveyard. I grew up on a farm 100 kilometres southwest of Dubbo. I am a sixth-generation farmer and have 3 siblings, 5 dogs, 2 cats, and heaps of sheep. My favourite subject is English.

My Hobbies

I have many hobbies that include rugby, riding, and repairing stuff. I’ve played rugby since I was five and started riding motorbikes around age 3 or 4. I’ve been repairing things for a long time and don’t remember when I started.

My Favourite Books

My favourite books include *The Hunger Games*, *The Maze Runner*, and *Powerless*. I enjoy them because they’re exciting and easy to connect with. I love reading and imagining myself in the story with the characters.
